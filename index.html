How to Use This Viewer
  • Loading Images:
    Drag and drop your numbered image sequence onto the page. Images should be named with a number at the end (e.g., "scan 1.jpg", "scan 2.jpg").
  • Viewing Images:
    Use the slider at the top to smoothly transition between images in the sequence.
  • Zooming:
    • • Use the + and - buttons
    • • Pinch gesture on touchpad/touchscreen
    • • Hold Ctrl/Cmd while scrolling
  • Panning:
    • • Click and drag with mouse
    • • Two-finger scroll on touchpad
    • • Drag with one finger on touchscreen
